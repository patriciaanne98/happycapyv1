We're a group of teens and college students who understand social anxiety firsthand. Together, we're building a world where every teen feels confident and supported.

Our Story

Imagine being 16 years old and feeling a wave of panic just thinking about raising your hand in class. Your heart races, your palms sweat, and your thoughts spiral with fear of being judged. Now imagine that's not just one day‚Äîit's every day.

For millions of teens, this is their reality. Social anxiety affects 1 in 3 teens in the U.S.‚Äîand nearly 1 in 4 in Arkansas. It disrupts friendships, school performance, and future opportunities.

As a team mostly made up of teens with two college students, we've all been affected by social anxiety. We understand the struggle because we've lived it. That's why we created HappyCapy‚Äînot as outsiders looking in, but as peers who get it.

We chose a capybara as our digital companion because they're naturally relaxed, friendly, and approachable creatures. Just like we want to be happy and calm like our mascot, HappyCapy represents the confidence and peace we're all working toward.
